The Dirac Field
String Theory


$$ \{\gamma^\mu, \gamma^\nu\} = 2\eta^{\mu \nu}\times\mathbb{I}_{n\times n} $$
Dirac Algebra

Where $n$ is the dimension of the matrices and $\mathbb{I}_{n\times n}$ the corresponding identity matrix.

The matrices that satisfy these anticommutation relations are reffered to as gamma or Dirac matrices.

$\eta^{\mu \nu} = \text{diag} (1,-1,-1,-1)$
